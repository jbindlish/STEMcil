Math Competitions

  • Math Kangaroo: This is an international math competition that is held annually for students in grades 1-12. The competition consists of multiple-choice questions and covers a range of math topics, including algebra, geometry, and number theory. The competition aims to encourage problem-solving and creative thinking skills in students.
  • The Noetic Learning Math Contest is an annual math competition for students in grades 2-8. The contest consists of 20 multiple-choice questions that cover a range of math topics, including algebra, geometry, and probability. The contest aims to develop problem-solving skills and inspire a love of math in students.
  • Math is Cool: Math is Cool is a math competition for students in grades 4-12 in the state of Washington. The competition consists of multiple-choice questions and covers a range of math topics, including algebra, geometry, and probability. The competition aims to encourage interest in math and develop problem-solving skills in students.
